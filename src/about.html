Opendisaster

Our entry project website for STI Baliuag's 1st term 2nd quarter i-Learn-Share(iLS).

What is Opendisaster?

Opendisaster is a website that aims to provide information about calamities and disasters. It also provides information about the different types of calamities and disasters, and how to prepare for them.

What is the purpose of Opendisaster?

Opendisaster aims to provide information about calamities and disasters, and how to prepare for them. It also aims to provide information about the different types of calamities and disasters.

What are the features of Opendisaster?

Opendisaster has a calamity guide that provides information about the different types of calamities and disasters, and how to prepare for them. It also has a calamity tracker that provides information about the current calamities and disasters that are happening in the Philippines.

What are the technologies used in making Opendisaster?

Opendisaster uses HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
